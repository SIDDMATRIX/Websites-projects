IILM University, Gurugram

Established in 2004, this campus is located on Golf Course Road in Gurugram. It offers modern infrastructure and strong industry connections.

IILM University, Greater Noida

Located in Knowledge Park, this 26-acre campus provides advanced facilities for engineering, management, and research programs.

IILM Lodhi Road, New Delhi

Established in 1993, this campus is known for business education and offers PGDM and BBA programs in the heart of Delhi.

IILM Academy of Higher Learning, Jaipur

Founded in 1998, the Jaipur campus focuses on management education with specializations like FinTech, Marketing, and Business Analytics.

IILM Academy of Higher Learning, Lucknow

Established in 2004, this campus offers PGDM and Finance programs with a strong focus on professional excellence.
